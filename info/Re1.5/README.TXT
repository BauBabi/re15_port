Important Notes:
-When you no longer have space in the inventory and need it, you only have to place the cursor on top of the item that you no longer need and hold the SELECT button, with this you will delete the selected item.
-When you play as Sherry try not to touch anything in the inventory (do not try to equip a weapon, since that will cause the game to crash!).
-When you equip an armor (R.P.D. or U.S.S.), for some strange reason, the game will automatically unequip the weapon that was being used at that time, however within the inventory you will see that it is equipped. The solution for this bug is as follows:
1-Enter the inventory.
2-Unequip the weapon you have equipped.
3-Get out of inventory.
4-Enter the inventory again and return to equip the weapon you want.
