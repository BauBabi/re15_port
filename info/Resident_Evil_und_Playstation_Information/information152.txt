PlayStation Sound Specs
PlayStation uses ADPCM sound

– 16-bit compressed down to 4
– Similar to SNES sound chip

Saving Main RAM

Use SsVABTransBodyPartly

PlayStation Developer’s Conference March 1996 TM
Transfer in background
SsVabTransCompleted
(SS_WAIT_COMPLETED) blocks until
the transfer is complete
SsVabTransCompleted
(SS_IMMEDIATE) returns immediately.
Returns 1 if the transfer is completed, 0
if it is ongoing
