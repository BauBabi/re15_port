the item model name would not get you anywhere if you plan to replace the gun with another item.

Extract the stars room .RDT (room1150.rdt or room1151.rdt for claire) and find the extracted folders in the same folder where your selected .RDT is located

"ROOM115&#092;PL00&#092;MODEL" or "ROOM115&#092;PL01&#092;MODEL"

inside this folder you will find MD1 models that can be converted to .TMD if necessary, find and identify the model you want to replace by looking at the equally named .TIM texture that is also located in the MODEL folder of the extracted .RDT

To swap models, find another model & it`s texture .TIM file you want to use by the same method (obviously search for models in other rooms) and just rename and replace them ;)

After that recompile the room/RDT with biofat :)
