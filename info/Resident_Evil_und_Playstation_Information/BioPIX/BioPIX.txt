BioHazard

PSone PIX Tool

MarkGrass
The-Horror.com
---------------------

Usage:
	<EXE> <PIX>

Example:
	BioPIX 


 - ver1.1 -
Extracts and converts PIX to Sony TIM

Files with the PIX extension found on various PSone versions
of BioHazard 1, 2 and 3 are RAW Sony TIMs. This program simply
extracts these RAW images and gives them a proper header.

This program was made for these files:

ITEMALL.PIX (Bio2)
ITEMG.PIX (Bio3)
ITEM_ALL.PIX (Bio1)
ITEM_MIX.PIX (Bio1)
MAP*.PIX (Bio2 Trial Edition)
MAPS.PIX (Bio2 Beta, Final Edition(s))
Any PIX file that is 150Kb in size
