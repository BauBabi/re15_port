TIM -Texture file. A varient of Sony's standard PSX TIM, info in the information section.

EMR - Animations. Don't know a lot

EMD/PLD - Houses several file types

TMD - Standard PSX model format

can be edited using Jum's E3d.

RE2 uses a varient of TMD and there is a tool to convert from a TMD to RE2's vairent.

Other than Jum's E3d there isn't a publically available tool to convert to tmd.
