This directory holds example source files for my PSX mipsgcc library
update.

The examples can be complied by running their respective batch files
(eg: to compile example6, just run example6.bat).
Note: the mipsgcc environment must be correct.

Example 1 - "play sample"
~~~~~~~~~
Shows how to upload a VAG sample to SPU RAM and play it.


Example 2 - "play sample with reverb"
~~~~~~~~~
Show how to upload sample and reverb data, and play a sample with
reverb.


Example 3 - "sample with reverb and ADSR envelope visualised"
~~~~~~~~~
The ADSR envelope for a voice is set, and a sample is played on that
voice. The current envelope amplitude is read from the SPU and 
graphed.


Example 4 - "play a MOD"
~~~~~~~~~
A music MOD is initialised and then played using the 50/60Hz vertical
blank sync.


Example 5 - "play samples from a VAB bank"
~~~~~~~~~
A VAB bank (sample bank) is uploaded into SPU RAM and the samples
therein are played.


Example 6 - "noise"
~~~~~~~~~
Plays a sound voice with noise mode set.


Example 7 - "PSX lib update test proggie"
~~~~~~~~~
Plays a MOD and dipslays text using the text-printing functions.
