spulib example 3

Upload sample, upload reverb, set ADSR, visualise ADSR.

Loads a looping VAG (sample) file.
Loads a reverb patch from the reverb patch bank.
Sets the ADSR envelope parameters.
Starts playing the sample.
Tracks and visualises the current ADSR envelope value.

Compile and link by running example3.bat (you may have to edit this
to put in the right include and library paths).


Note: Each voice has it's own volume envelope (ADSR envelope), which
defines how the volume of the voice changes over time.

Sample made with TS404 :)
